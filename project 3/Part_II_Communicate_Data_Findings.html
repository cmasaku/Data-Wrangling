Part II - Effects Borrower Characteristics to Loan Status¶

by Carolyne Masaku¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I was focussing on finding out if a borrower's characteristics affects their Loan Status. The main focus was on these features: CreditGrade, EmploymentStatus, BorrowerAPR,CurrentDelinquencies, IncomeRange, IncomeVerifiable, LoanOriginalAmount, Term, IsBorrowerHomeOwner, MonthlyLoanPayment.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset consisted of 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others. 61 of the variables are numeric in nature.
